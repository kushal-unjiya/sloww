## Design Context

### Users
Researchers, analysts, and knowledge workers who upload private documents and need **answers they can trust**, with explicit provenance. They use Sloww when depth matters more than speed-of-hype.

### Brand Personality
**Grounded · sharp · trustworthy** — confident typography and contrast, no playful gimmicks. The product should feel like serious tooling, not a toy chatbot.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Dark, high-contrast workspace** with a **single electric violet** accent (actions, focus, citations). References favor split auth marketing + form, dense data table for “Your Data,” and a two-pane chat with a persistent rail. **Email OTP only** in V1 (no Google OAuth in UI). Avoid cyan–purple gradients, glassmorphism, and gradient metric heroes (generic AI chrome).

### Design Principles
1. **One accent color** owns interactive emphasis; status colors are secondary and semantic only.
2. **Hierarchy through scale and weight**, not decoration — big headlines, quiet metadata.
3. **Sidebar is the spine** — navigation, uploads entry, and identity stay fixed; content scrolls independently.
4. **Tables are scannable** — sticky header, zebra-none but row hover, tight rhythm.
5. **Respect reduced motion** — prefer opacity/transform for micro-motion; no elastic easing.
